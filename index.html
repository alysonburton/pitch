Problem

Everyday new vegan businesses are popping up here in LA and all over the world, businesses that often either through lack of funds or lack of knowledge end up with very poor branding and websites.

These businesses are often started by people who are foreign to our market, and usually know nothing about branding, typography or websites and end up muddling through it themselves, and thus missing out on the amount of business that a good branding and marketing strategy can bring in. And because of this, a lot of them fail, because no one even knew they existed.
What if there was a vegan design firm that came to them and worked to help them succeed? The trust would already be there, we'd all be working towards the same goal and with our help they'd reach a bigger audience, set up a professional brand and a website that attracted customers.

Solution

A design firm of vegans for vegans that provides all the marketing, branding, web design and development services needed to help start and grow a business.

Business Model con't

  • Start local
  • Approach new and current vegan businesses offering our services
  • Slowly build up to a point in which we can rent a physical local and start hiring more staff
  • Get to the point where the design firm is running sucessfully on its own, then branch out to Portland and finally a remote firm employing vegans all over the world
We plan to file for an LLC in December and launch in January
It should take about a year to get enough steady business to afford physical location. We'll spend the next year increasing staff and business, and we estimate that in 3-4 years the firm should be self-sufficient, meaning I'll have found a business manager I can trust to run the LA firm in which time I will be moving to Portland to open a second location.
Finally once that's steady, we will open up employment worldwide and set up a base of vegan designers, developers and marketing experts to work with vegan businesses not located in LA or Portland.

Financial Projections & Key metrics

  • Start by splitting profit 3 ways
  • As business grows, hire a Finance Manager
  • Competitive Salary for employees with benefits
  • Pricing: Start case by case, then develop packages based on the demand we see
I don't imagine there being much of a cost at the beginning, aside from the LLC paperwork.
Once we start getting enough work to increase our staff and obtain a location, we will hire a finance manager to handle payment of employees, and business costs bc that is not our expertise
We will pay our employees well and provide benefits such as health insurance, which will actually be cheaper since everyone will be vegan we'll also work with our clientel to get our employees additional benefits at their facilites, like meal discounts which will bring in more business to our clients as well as providing some exclusive benefits to working at our design firm
for pricing we'll start out on a case by case basis and as we continue to grow our clientel we'll develop packages based on their most common needs
